About Us

Welcome to Mulchand Mithaiwale

At Mulchand Mithaiwale, we believe in spreading happiness through our delectable range of sweets. Established in 2000, we have been serving the Pune community with the finest quality sweets and desserts for over two decades.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create sweets that not only tantalize the taste buds but also evoke fond memories and bring people together. What started as a small shop in the heart of Pune has now grown into a beloved destination for sweet lovers across the city.

Our Philosophy

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. We meticulously select the finest ingredients, handcraft each delicacy with care, and uphold the highest standards of hygiene and freshness. Our commitment to quality ensures that every bite of our sweets is a delightful experience.

Our Range

From traditional classics like Gulab Jamun and Rasgulla to innovative creations like Chocolate Barfi and Mango Sandesh, our diverse range of sweets offers something for every palate. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a sweet treat, we have the perfect indulgence for you.

Our Team

Behind every delicious sweet is a team of passionate individuals dedicated to excellence. From our skilled chefs who masterfully blend flavors to our friendly staff who provide exceptional service, each member of the Mulchand Mithaiwale family plays a vital role in our success.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit our store and experience the magic of Mulchand Mithaiwale firsthand. Whether you're a long-time patron or a first-time visitor, we look forward to welcoming you with open arms and sweet smiles.
